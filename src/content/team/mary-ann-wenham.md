**Mary-Ann Wenham, tour guide and ex-teacher:**

As a former History and English teacher, I bring a deep passion for storytelling to our tours. My goal is to make history accessible and engaging, connecting the past to the present through the vibrant stories of Cape Town and its people.

With years of experience in education, I specialize in crafting tours that are both informative and inspiring, ensuring every guest leaves with a deeper understanding of our city's heritage.
