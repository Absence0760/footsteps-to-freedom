Cape Town – Nelson Mandela's home for three decades – played a pivotal role in his journey from prisoner to president. The city has a captivating Mandela story to tell as it provided the setting for the unfolding of a series of significant and secretive meetings that led to the surprise release of Mandela and the unbanning of the ANC in February 1990.
