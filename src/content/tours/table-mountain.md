Guests have the option of either hiking up Table Mountain (enquire about the different route options) or taking the cable car up. If taking the cable car, we pre-purchase tickets for our guests to avoid the queues (which can be long in the busy seasons).

Once ascended, there are many splendid views from the various look-out points of the ocean and the magnificent vistas beyond. If guests enjoy walking and are wearing comfortable walking shoes, there is an easy and interesting walk to Maclear's Beacon which is the highest point of Table Mountain and is still used by cartographers today. Besides giving you a different perspective of the mountain and the city, the walk takes you through the unique Table Mountain fynbos.

Hike and conquer one of the world's seven natural wonders, Table Mountain. Accompanied by an experienced mountain guide, take one of the most popular hiking routes to the summit of this incredible natural wonder. Walk to the highest point in the Table Mountain range, Maclear's Beacon and wander through the majestic Cape Floral Kingdom before descending in the cable car. Round trip takes 3-4 hours depending on your level of fitness.

Please note this tour is only for those who live active lives and have a good level of fitness.
