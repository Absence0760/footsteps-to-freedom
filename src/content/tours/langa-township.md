Townships were created during the years of apartheid, as racially divided residential areas on the outskirts of South Africa cities. In the midst of hardship and challenges, you will be inspired by stories and people of amazing courage, resourcefulness and pride. Experience the warmth and vibrancy of the Xhosa people as you encounter everyday life on the bustling streets of Langa.
