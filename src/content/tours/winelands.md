Your guide will collect you for your accommodation and travel to the Cape Winelands areas of Stellenbosch, Paarl and Franschhoek. Depending on your personal interests, you will have a day exploring wines, gardens, Cape Dutch architecture, cheeses, craft beer, artisanal chocolate, or a collection of rare motor vehicles housed on a magnificent wine estate.

Whilst your route will always be planned according to your particular wine preferences (and we would love to know what they are before your arrival in Cape Town), some of our favourite spots include de Morgenzon and Ken Forrester for their chenin blancs; Meerlust and Ernie Els for their big bold reds, Kanonkop for South Africa's own unique pinotage variety and Anthonij Rupert for their delicious champagne and nougat pairing.

Lunch will be somewhere in the winelands – so many wonderful places to choose from, depending on whether you feel like a light deli lunch or a more substantial à la carte menu. After lunch, you will head to the historical university campus town of Stellenbosch to explore and perhaps have a coffee on a sidewalk café.
