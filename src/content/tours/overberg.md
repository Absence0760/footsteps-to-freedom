Hermanus is a Cape southern coast holiday town and is famous for its Southern Right whale watching (best time to see whales: August – October), which you can view either from a boat or from the well-loved cliff walks. Get to know your 'whale jargon' beforehand so you can recognize 'spy hopping' from 'lob tailing' from 'breaching'!

Our tour also includes a visit to the surrounding wine areas - the beautiful Hemel en Aarde Valley where you can taste some of the finest South African wines and food.
